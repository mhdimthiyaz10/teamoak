Team Oak Tobacco
Wholesale Trading

Established in 2000, Team Oak Trading Company LLC is a leading tobacco wholesale distributor based in Makkah and Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With over two decades of industry experience, the company has built a strong reputation for reliability, product quality, and professional service, becoming a trusted partner for retailers and distributors across the region.

We specialize in the wholesale distribution of premium tobacco products sourced from internationally recognized manufacturers and authorized suppliers, ensuring authenticity, consistent quality, and compliance with Saudi market regulations. Our efficient supply chain and organized distribution network enable timely deliveries and dependable product availability to meet customer demands.

Customer satisfaction remains central to our operations. Through transparent business practices, personalized service, and a knowledgeable team with deep industry expertise, we maintain long-term relationships with our clients while adapting to evolving market needs. Committed to ethical business practices and full regulatory compliance, Team Oak Trading Company LLC continues to strengthen its position as a reliable and preferred wholesale partner within the Saudi tobacco market.
