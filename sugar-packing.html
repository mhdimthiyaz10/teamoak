Industrial Facilities

Sugar Packing Unit
& Distribution

Precision in Every Package

A sugar packing unit is a facility where bulk sugar received from sugar factories or suppliers is weighed, packed, and prepared for distribution or export.

In countries like Saudi Arabia (KSA), these units play an important role in the food supply chain because large quantities of sugar are imported and then repacked for supermarkets, restaurants, and industries.

Ensuring precision and quality in every package, we serve as a crucial link in the food supply chain for supermarkets, restaurants, and industries globally.
